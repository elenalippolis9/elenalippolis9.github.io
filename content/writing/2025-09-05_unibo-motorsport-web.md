Building and Managing a Formula SAE Team Website
Published: 2025-09-05

Between 2021 and 2022 I was the web developer for UNIBO Motorsport,
the Formula SAE team at the University of Bologna. I managed both
the team site and sponsor pages as part of the Marketing team.

It was my first real-world web role — tight deadlines, stakeholder
feedback from sponsors, and learning fast what actually matters
when people outside your bubble are reading your work.
