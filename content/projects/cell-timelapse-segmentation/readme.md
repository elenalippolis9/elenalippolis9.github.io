Image Segmentation of Cellular Timelapse Microscopy
BSc Thesis · University of Bologna · 2023
Internship at CASY, in collaboration with Rizzoli Orthopedic Institute

Automated processing and segmentation pipeline for time-lapse
microscopy images of healthy and cancerous cells. Built a pre-processing
pipeline with noise reduction and contrast enhancement, improving
downstream cell delineation versus unprocessed inputs.

Designed for extensibility as a reusable tool for biomedical
image analysis in orthopaedic research contexts.

stack: Python, OpenCV
